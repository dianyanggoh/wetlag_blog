Crafting the Identity of Android: Netrunner

Sep 30, 2015  

Video: https://vimeo.com/125164271

I have the base game and a few expansion packs and no one to play with.

I like how he describes the process by which he went around redesigning the original Netrunner. THe choices he made. The things he kept. The things he discarded.

Around 28:00 , he talks about mixing factions. he starts talking about Influence and Blue Moon. He steals the blue moon mechanic. He makes the comparison to gift cards. How you’re inclined to spend it once you have it. I think it’s a brilliant insight. Game design is understanding human nature. Encourage players in a subtle way.
