Feedback for Swipe Input

Nov 23, 2015   #controls  #feedback 

I tried this game Heatos on my phone the other day, which involved swiping tiles around on a grid.

The tiles only moved after you’ve completed a swipe.

Threes handles swipe input really well. The moment you start a swipe, the tiles move a little. You can slide your thumb or finger back and forth until you’ve decided to commit to a move, and the tiles will move in corresponding fashion. Beyond providing feedback, it doubles as a preview of your intended action.

Even when tiles can’t be moved, there’s a subtle stretch and squash effect to indicate so.

The point is, there’s feedback throughout the entire duration of a swipe, not just at the end.
